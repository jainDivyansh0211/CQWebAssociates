My 3 Favourite Places in IIT Kharagpur

Technology Student's Gymkhana

This place lies in heart of every KGPian.

The Technology Students' Gymkhana is a popular place for students because it's where they can do fun activities, cultural events, and sports. It's a cool spot where students can be creative and hang out with friends, making it a favorite among everyone.

Click here to visit Gymkhana website

Nehru Museum of Science and Technology

Loved because it's a cool place to learn interesting things about the past. It's like a fun history adventure!

Nehru Museum at IIT Kharagpur is a perfect hangout for friends, offering a relaxed atmosphere where you can enjoy interesting exhibits and spend quality time together, making memories outside of classes.

Visit website of Nehru Museum

Kalidas Auditorium


A place for all the shows and fun times with friends!

Kalidas Auditorium is famous for its vibrant events, lively performances, and a welcoming ambiance. It's the go-to place for entertainment, fostering a sense of community and shared experiences among the student body.
